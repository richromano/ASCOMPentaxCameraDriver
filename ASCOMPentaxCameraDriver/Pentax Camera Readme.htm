Pentax KP ASCOM Camera Driver

This is an ASCOM driver for Pentax KP cameras. The driver should work with any Pentax KP and K1 that connects to the Image Transmitter 2 application in USB mode.

This driver was developed on Windows 10, with a Pentax KP camera.

I'd like to thank all of the people who have helped test the driver, and who have contributed their camera profiles so that the driver will work with more models.

Documentation
